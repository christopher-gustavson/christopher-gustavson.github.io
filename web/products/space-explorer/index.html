Focal Length: 1000mm
Mount Type: Computerized Altazimuth

Experience the Cosmos Like Never Before

The VoltTech Space Explorer sets a new standard for amateur astronomy. Its precision-engineered optics deliver remarkable clarity, crisp contrast, and pinpoint sharpness across the entire field of view.

Key Features

Our 90mm aperture gathers 73% more light than our previous 70mm model, revealing fainter deep-space objects and greater planetary detail. The fully multi-coated optics system provides enhanced light transmission for brighter, clearer, sharper views.

Smart Technology Integration

The included smartphone adapter transforms your Space Explorer into an astrophotography rig. Capture breathtaking images of the Moon, Jupiter's Galilean moons, Saturn's rings, deep-sky objects, and more. Share your galactic discoveries with friends and fellow astronomy enthusiasts.

Portable Yet Powerful

Despite its professional-grade capabilities, the Space Explorer breaks down for convenient transport. The tripod collapses to just 60cm for easy storage, and the optical tube weighs only 3.5kg, making it ideal for backyard sessions or dark sky excursions.
